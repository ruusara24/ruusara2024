About Us

"Coinciding with "Let My Country Awake,"the student's festival of literature,the Literary Subcommittee of the University of Peradeniya is organizing the "Rusara Digital Creation Competition"for the fourth time.The competition will be held under the main categories of dubbing,photo manipulation,character design,and book trailer showdown."

Objectives

  1. Enhance technical and aesthetic knowledge and interest in digital art.
  2. Develop creativity.
  3. Improve skills related to digital art.
  4. Appreciate and enjoy various forms of digital art.
  5. Introduce and encourage young creators.
