About Us

"Coinciding with the "Awaken My Country" student literary festival, the Literary Subcommittee of the University of Peradeniya is organizing the "Rusara Digital Creation Competition" for the fourth time. The competition will be held under the main categories of voice recordings, photo editing, digital character illustrations, and video creation related to the story context of a selected book."

Objectives

  1. Enhance technical and aesthetic knowledge and interest in digital art.
  2. Develop creativity.
  3. Improve skills related to digital art.
  4. Appreciate and enjoy various forms of digital art.
  5. Introduce and encourage young creators.
