The competition rules and regulation

All participants in the competition must thoroughly read and understand all the competition rules and regulations.

  • Before uploading, you must register through our website and obtain a registration number. (Current updated will be notified via WhatsApp).
  • You must register on the website and obtain a number before the specified date.
  • Participants must submit their creations online before the due date.
  • Each participant is allowed to submit only one creation.
  • The original photos used for the creation must be submitted.
  • AI Generators are not allowed.
  • All copyright responsibilities of the original photos lie with the creator.
  • The maximum number of original photos that can be used is 8.
  • Creations can be submitted in JPEG or PNG format.
  • Participants have the freedom to submit creations that convey an important message to society.
  • Creations containing political or religious content will not be accepted for evaluation and will be removed from the competition.
  • A brief description of the message conveyed by your creation, not exceeding 50 words, must be submitted as a PDF file.
  • All submitted creations must not contain any logos, copyright marks, identifying marks, borders, or any other type of markings.
  • The decision of the judging panel is final.
  • We plan to conduct online training workshops for this competition category, which will be held during the period of receiving the creations.
  • The competition consists of two categories: the most creative and the most popular creation.
  • The most creative videos will be selected by the judging panel. The best 10 creative videos will be posted on our Facebook page, and the most popular creation will be selected based on Facebook likes.
  • The original photos should be clearly mentioned in one file, the final creation in another file, and the brief description in another separate file. All these files should be renamed with the registration number and uploaded as a single zip/rar file.
  • During the upload, it is essential to provide several screenshots of the layers of your creation along with the submission.
  • Before uploading, fill out the Google Form available on our website and upload the zip file through it.
