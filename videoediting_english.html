The competition rules and regulation

All participants in the competition must thoroughly read and understand all the competition rules and regulations.

  • Before uploading, you must register through our website and obtain a registration number (current updated will be communicated via WhatsApp).
  • You must register on the website and obtain a number before the specified date.
  • Participants must submit their creations electronically before the due date.
  • At any time, you must be prepared to provide the original files of your creation (do not submit the source file during upload).
  • Each participant can submit only one creation for the competition.
  • Your video script must be your original work.
  • You should present the context or summary of the book you select creatively. This must be an original creation you make based on a video clip you create or a video clip you obtain from the internet.
  • Under this competition section, you are required to create a video clip presenting the summary of the relevant story of the selected book.
  • You can create a video (trailer) for any genre of story you choose (mystery, romance, fantasy, etc.).
  • You can choose to include voiceovers or not based on your preference.
  • Political slander, racist content should not be included. Videos containing such content will be disqualified.
  • If voiceovers are used, obscene, abusive, or harsh words should not be used. Videos containing such audio will be disqualified.
  • The original video clips used for the creation must be submitted.
  • The creator is responsible for all copyright matters related to the original video clips used.
  • Videos must be submitted in mp4 format. You can use any frame rate you prefer for 1920x1080 resolution.
  • The total duration of the video should be less than 2 minutes.
  • All submitted creations should not contain any logos, copyright marks, identifying marks, borders, or any other marks.
  • The uploaded video size should be less than 500MB.
  • To allow for your creativity, we do not impose any restrictions on the software you use to create your creation (AI generators are not allowed).
  • A brief description of the story in the video, not exceeding 60 words, should be submitted as a PDF file.
  • The competition consists of two categories: the most creative and the most popular creation.
  • The creative videos will be selected by the judging panel, and the top 10 most creative videos will be posted on our Facebook page, from which the most popular creation will be selected by Facebook likes.
  • The final decision will be given by the judging panel.
  • We plan to hold online training workshops during the creation submission period.
  • Your creation must be uploaded as a single mp4 file, along with a brief description (name and a short description about the story) in a PDF file, with all the aforementioned files renamed using the registration number as a single zip/rar file.
  • When uploading your creation, it is mandatory to upload several screenshots of the layers of your creation along with it.
  • Before uploading, you must fill out the Google form on our website and upload the zip file through it.
