CHARACTER DESIGNING

The competition rules and regulation

All participants in the competition must thoroughly read and understand all the competition rules and regulations.

  • Before uploading, you must register through our website and obtain a registration number. (Current updated will be notified via WhatsApp).
  • You must register on the website and obtain a number before the due date.
  • Participants must submit their creations online before the specified date.
  • Be prepared to provide the original files of your creation at any time.
  • Each participant is allowed to submit only one creation.
  • The design must be presented under the theme 'A fictional superhero in Sri Lanka during the Ravana era'.
  • It is mandatory to submit a full-figure drawing of the character you are presenting.
  • If there is a background creation, it must be designed in a way that does not obstruct the depiction of the relevant character.
  • The design can be created using 3D drawing or 2D drawing software, but the final result must be presented in a 2D format.
  • To allow for your creativity, we do not impose restrictions on the software you use to create your design. You can use any software (AI generators are not allowed).
  • Your creation can be submitted in JPEG or PNG format. The resolution of the creation should be higher than 960px x 1280px, and the file size should be less than 20MB.
  • The submitted creation must be your own. If it is traced or confirmed to be someone else's creation, you will be disqualified from the competition.
  • Political, religious, or socially harmful creations will not be accepted for evaluation and will be removed from the competition.
  • All submitted creations must not contain any logos, copyright marks, or any other identifying marks, except for the identity of the character (name and description can be included within the creation).
  • The competition consists of two categories: most creative and most popular creation.
  • The most creative images will be selected by the judging panel. The best 10 creative images will be posted on our Facebook page, and the most popular creation will be selected based on Facebook likes.
  • The decision of the judging panel is final.
  • We plan to conduct online training workshops for this competition category, which will be held during the period of receiving the creations.
  • Uploading the source file of the design is mandatory.
  • Your design must be uploaded as a single file in JPEG or PNG format, the source file of your design as a single file, and a short description not exceeding 50 words (character name and description, including his abilities, powers, etc.) as a PDF. All these files must be clearly named with the registration number and uploaded as a single zip/rar file.
  • During the upload, it is essential to provide several screenshots of the layers of your creation along with the submission.
  • Before uploading, fill out the Google Form available on our website and upload the zip file through it.
