DUBBING

The competition rules and regulation

All participants in the competition must thoroughly read and understand all the competition rules and regulations.

  • Before uploading, you must register through our website and obtain a registration number. (Timely information will be notified via WhatsApp).
  • You must register on the website and obtain a number before the due date.
  • Participants must submit their creations online before the specified date.
  • Your voice-over script must be your original work.
  • You can do voice-overs for any 2 videos of your choice through cartoon character voices or human character voices.
  • Videos should not be edited or altered in any way.
  • A team can submit only 2 creations.
  • A team can have a maximum of 5 members.
  • Political insults and racist remarks should not be included. If such remarks are included, the video will be disqualified.
  • Profane, abusive, and harsh words should not be used. If such remarks are included, the video will be disqualified.
  • The size of the uploaded video should be less than 500MB.
  • Videos must be submitted in mp4 format. Any frame rate can be used as long as the resolution is 1920x1080.
  • The total duration of each video must be less than 1 minute.
  • All submitted videos should not contain any logos, copyright marks, identifying marks, borders, or any other types of markings.
  • The competition consists of two categories: Most Creative and Most Popular.
  • The creative videos will be selected by the judging panel, and the best 10 creative videos will be published on our Facebook page, where the most popular creation will be chosen by Facebook likes.
  • The decision of the judging panel is final.
  • Online training sessions are expected to be held for this competition category, and they will be held during the creation submission period.
  • When awarding marks, the judging panel will mainly consider:

    • a. The creativity of the video.

    b. Voice matching and control according to the character.

    c. The flow of the video and dialogue.

    d. The meaningfulness and coherence of the video dialogue.

  • The final creation must be uploaded as a single zip/rar file named after the registration number.
  • Before uploading, you must fill out the Google form on our website and upload the zip file through it.
